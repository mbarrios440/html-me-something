My cats!

Chibi

He's a super sweet chunky boi, and also othe most independent between the two of them. Most of the time he'll be found sleeping on one of their squishy beds or eating.

Copper

This one's a big attention seeker! He's always ready to play and a little crazy at times(specially in the mornings). He always wants to cuddle with us when he's sleepy.

I used to be the type of person that would rather not have any pets, but after a little convincing from my girlfriend, we ended up adopting these two brothers, and I can say it's been one of the best decisions I've made. They make our home feel more like a home and honestly I couldn't imagine going back to the quietness before we had them.
